# Stereotype & Identity Humor Examples Library

## Allowed Examples
- "My ADHD is making me a professional tab-hoarder." (self-referential, speaker owns identity)
- "We're all just neurodivergent gremlins here." (community-shared, in-group)
- "I'm a switch, so I can't even decide on a sandwich." (kink/sexual archetype, self-directed)
- "Creators are all just caffeine-fueled goblins." (creator stereotype, general satire)
- "My trauma response is to meme about it." (self-directed trauma humor)

## Needs Context / Mod Review Examples
- "That's the most aggressively bisexual outfit I've ever seen." (sexuality stereotype, context-dependent)
- "You're so autistic about spreadsheets." (neurodiversity stereotype, context-dependent)
- "That's some light kink-shaming, but it's funny." (kink, context-dependent)
- "All programmers are socially awkward." (general stereotype, context-dependent)

## Not Allowed Examples
- "All [racial group] are lazy." (identity-based harassment, racism)
- "You're too autistic to understand this." (weaponizing neurodiversity trait)
- "He is a woman." (malicious misgendering)
- "Your trauma is showing, freak." (trauma-weaponizing)
- "Using slurs with stereotypes."
- "Threats or targeted harassment."
