# Neunuc Platform: Pre-Launch Community & Hype Strategy

A strategic pre-launch campaign is crucial for building momentum, gathering early feedback, and ensuring a strong user base from day one. Based on the Neunuc platform's unique identity—AI-driven, neurodivergent-first, and focused on deep intellectual engagement—this plan builds buzz and cultivates a dedicated community.

## Overview

This plan focuses on building an engaged community and generating authentic interest in the **four weeks leading up to the official deployment**.

---

## Phase 1: Foundation & Initial Outreach (T-8 to T-5 Weeks)

This phase lays the groundwork for the community and prepares initial content.

### T-8: Strategy & Platform Setup

**Key Focus:** Establish foundation

**Key Activities:**
- **Finalize Pre-Launch Narrative**: Define core message focusing on:
  - "A new era of neuro-inclusive AI"
  - "Tools for profound thinking"
- **Set up Private Discord Server**: Create channels:
  - `#announcements`
  - `#introductions`
  - `#feedback-and-ideas`
  - Specialized channels for features:
    - `#domino-planner-strategies`
    - `#cq-model-ethics`

### T-7: "Genesis" Content Creation

**Key Focus:** Foundational content

**Key Activities:**
- **Write Core Blog Posts**: Develop foundational articles:
  - "Why We're Building a Neurodivergent-First AI"
  - "Beyond Tomfoolery: Introducing the Critique Resonance Chamber"
  - "What is a 'Covert Quotient'?"
- **Prepare Teaser Visuals**: Create:
  - Short, intriguing video clips
  - Animated GIFs showcasing:
    - Domino Planner in action
    - Dialectic Dashboard features

### T-6: Identify "Founding Members"

**Key Focus:** Strategic outreach

**Key Activities:**
- **Curate Invitation List**: Identify 50-100 key individuals from:
  - r/artificial
  - r/ADHD_Programmers
  - LinkedIn groups (AI ethics, system thinkers, neurodiversity advocates)
- **Draft Personalized Invitations**: Emphasize:
  - Their expertise
  - Value of early feedback
  - "Founding Member" status

### T-5: "Founding Member" Onboarding

**Key Focus:** High-touch engagement

**Key Activities:**
- **Send Invitations**: Begin personalized outreach
- **Engage Directly**: 
  - Personal greetings in `#introductions`
  - Ask about their work
  - Discuss Neunuc excitement
  - Create premium, high-touch feel

---

## Phase 2: Community Cultivation & Content Seeding (T-4 to T-1 Weeks)

Nurture early community and build public anticipation.

### T-4: Exclusive Content Drops & Public Teasers

**Key Focus:** Launch public presence

**Key Activities:**
- **Discord Exclusives**:
  - Share first "Genesis" blog post
  - Drop UI teaser video
  - Solicit direct feedback
- **Public Launch of Landing Page**:
  - Compelling headline
  - Brief Neunuc overview
  - Single CTA: "Join the Waitlist"
- **First Public Teaser**:
  - Post teaser on X/Twitter, LinkedIn
  - Link to landing page

### T-3: Deep Dives & Waitlist Growth

**Key Focus:** Transparency and engagement

**Key Activities:**
- **Host Founder's AMA**: 
  - Text-based in private Discord
  - Transparent about vision and challenges
- **Publish First Blog Post Publicly**:
  - Release first "Genesis" article
  - Medium or company blog
  - Drive traffic from social media
  - Encourage waitlist sign-ups

### T-2: Feature Spotlight & Interactive Engagement

**Key Focus:** Interactive demonstrations

**Key Activities:**
- **"Critique Chamber" Challenge**:
  - Post interesting text in Discord
  - Ask Founding Members to critique
  - Share Critique Resonance Chamber analysis
  - Compare community vs. AI critiques
- **Publicly Share Second Blog Post**:
  - Article about Critique Resonance Chamber
  - Explain "intellectual tomfoolery" concept

### T-1: Final Countdown & Hype Building

**Key Focus:** Launch momentum

**Key Activities:**
- **Announce Official Launch Date**:
  - Notify Discord community
  - Alert public waitlist
- **"Early Access" for Founding Members**:
  - Grant access 24-48 hours pre-launch
  - Rewards early involvement
  - Catch last-minute bugs
- **Final Teaser Blitz**:
  - Social media countdown
  - Share final teaser visuals
  - Build excitement

## Success Metrics

### Community Engagement
- Discord member count
- Daily active users in Discord
- Message volume and quality
- Founding Member participation rate

### Public Interest
- Waitlist sign-ups
- Landing page traffic
- Social media engagement (likes, shares, comments)
- Blog post views and shares

### Content Performance
- Teaser video views
- Blog post engagement metrics
- Community feedback quality
- Feature-specific interest levels

---

## Key Takeaways

This pre-launch strategy is designed to:

1. **Build Strong Foundation**: Cultivate loyal community from inception
2. **Generate Authentic Interest**: Focus on genuine engagement over hype
3. **Gather Early Feedback**: Use Founding Members for product refinement
4. **Create Momentum**: Ensure active user base at launch
5. **Establish Identity**: Reinforce neurodivergent-first, intellectually rigorous brand

When Neunuc goes live, there will already be an active, engaged user base ready to explore and champion the platform.
