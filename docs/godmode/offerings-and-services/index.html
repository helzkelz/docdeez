Offerings and Services

HelenKella is a neurodivergent-first operating system and ecosystem designed to empower creators, survivors, and builders through consent-based autonomy, adaptive intelligence, and safe chaos. Below is a comprehensive breakdown of our offerings and services, organized by category.

1. Core Platform Offerings — HelenKella OS​

The foundational "OS for the Beautifully Broken," providing personalized, adaptive tools for daily life and creativity.

  • ApexCore (Backend Orchestrator): Workflow management, permission contracts, cross-platform sync, and enterprise licensing. Supports 100K+ concurrent users with real-time feedback latency under 500ms.
  • VigilEye (Adaptive Dashboard): Personalized UI by archetype (Chaos Mode, Support Mode, Focus Mode), real-time chaos quotient display, consent chip visibility, and budget pill alerts. Includes gauges for energy, focus, and revenue tracking.
  • NexusWidget (Discord Bot + Games): MBTI role sync, event-based games, GenAI tasks, in-game economy tied to marketplace, and multiplayer flashcards. Enables community rituals and gamification.

Pricing: Free tier (100–200 steps/mo), Starter ($19/mo for 2K–5K steps), Pro ($99/mo for 20K steps), Enterprise (custom).

2. Specialized Tools​

Modular extensions for specific use cases, built on the core OS.

  • VoiceForge (Voice Agent): AI-powered voice commands for local Ollama or cloud models, CI/CD triggers via speech, biometric authentication, and voice-triggered automations.
  • MysConduct (Creator Compliance): Immutable consent ledger for NSFW assets, age-gating, content vault, compliance flows, and legal guardrails. Designed for ethical adult creators.
  • Satirium (Content Engine): Rapid satire/zine creation with ethics checkpoints, "Vibe Wrangler" agent for tone adaptation, and legal guardrails built-in.
  • MysManaged (Business Suite): ND-friendly timeboxing, POS integration, invoicing, grant compliance packs, and SOP templates for SMB operations.
  • GigWeave (Marketplace): Paid skills, microtools, workshops, coaching, and peer-led mentorship. 80%+ creator payout (vs. 20% platform fee).

Pricing: Integrated into OS tiers; add-ons start at $10/mo per tool.

3. Creator & Community Services​

Empowerment-focused features for building and monetizing communities.

  • Branding & Strategy Services: Brand kits, meme kits, content prompts, collaboration matching, and coaching. Includes upgrade tiers with visibility boosts.
  • Gamified Events: Sh*tpost Olympics (meme contests), Chaos Collabs (paired creator drops), Mood Wars (themed content), Sounds + Sins (music + NSFW streams), and Quiet Takeovers (sensory-safe days).
  • Mental Health & ND-Friendly UX: Chaos/Calm feed switching, Quiet Mode for burnout, no leaderboards, mood-tagged filters, and sensory-safe interactions.
  • Safe Spaces: The Vault (NSFW creator space), The Commons (SFW artist space), The Park (games and rest), The Engine (contribution hub), and The Stage (creator spotlight).

Pricing: Free access to basics; event access via pay-per-event ($5–$50) or subscriptions.

4. Monetization & Revenue Services​

Direct paths to income for creators and users.

  • Subscription Bundles: SFW/NSFW access tiers, pay-per-event monetization, and creator services (marketing, branding, strategy).
  • Marketplace & Commissions: 20% platform commission on paid extensions; Rituals-as-a-Service ($49–$199 for unlockable kits).
  • Professional Services: Consulting ($100–$250/hr or $3K–$20K/project), workshops, and digital products (stickers, zines, holiday bundles).
  • Donations & Merch: Ko-fi/Patreon tiers, referral bonuses, and merch drops (e.g., Pattern-Aware: Stars, Scars, and Soft Resistance).

Pricing: Variable; e.g., merch bundles at $20–$100, subscriptions $7–$49/mo.

5. Legal, Ethical, and Advocacy Offerings​

Tools for rights, safety, and empowerment.

  • Know-Your-Rights Tools: State-by-state rights checker for civil issues, DCFS/custody guidance, and violation identification. Guides users through rights without giving legal advice.
  • Legal Ritual Armory: Customizable templates for custody, advocacy, and documentation. Includes peer-led mentorship and local resource locators.
  • NSFW Ethics & Compliance: Age verification, consent-ledger transparency, trauma-informed onboarding, and anti-exploitation policies.
  • Policy & Mental Health Integration: Frameworks for neurodivergence liability shielding, LLC formation, TOS, privacy policy, and IP protection.

Pricing: Free tier for basics; premium access ($29/mo) for advanced tools.

6. Technical & Infrastructure Services​

Backend and deployment support for scaling.

  • HelenCore / HKDevOps Studio: Production-grade AI orchestration with KEDA autoscaling, guardrails, LangGraph checkpointing, and observability. Supports 1M+ API requests/hour with 88%+ ROI.
  • Voice Orchestrator: Dual-agent system (computer-use + browser) with persistent memory, adaptive personality, and auto-reports.
  • Firebase + GCP Deployment: Multi-environment hosting, CI/CD pipelines, secret management, caching, and API hub integration.
  • Local AI Options: Ollama-based models for privacy-first operations; choice between local and cloud inference.

Pricing: Included in Pro/Enterprise tiers; custom deployments $3K–$20K.

Guiding Principles & Mission Alignment​

All offerings adhere to core principles: "Your Body, Your Data" (privacy-first), "Powered by Your Brain" (adaptive intelligence), "Safe Chaos is Good Chaos" (trauma-informed design), and "Community Over Algorithm" (contribution-based visibility). The mission is to create a neurodivergent-safe, sex-positive, creativity-centered space where visibility is earned through contribution, not exploitation.
