Cognitive & Culture Sub-systems

This page breaks down the major sub-systems of the HelenKella Godmode System and their responsibilities.

Bethy: AI Voice Orchestrator​

Bethy is the primary interface & orchestrator.

Responsibilities​

  • Receive user input (voice, chat, CLI, Discord, etc.)
  • Parse intent, emotional tone, and urgency
  • Route requests to:
    • Synaptic Cortex Nexus
    • Neunuc ARR Loop
    • Neuro-Navigator
    • Integration workers
  • Maintain conversational state and context
  • Apply Humor SOP and Ethos Navigator constraints before responding

Bethy is not just a chat UI. It is an orchestration layer that turns human intent into structured system actions.

Synaptic Cortex Nexus​

The Synaptic Cortex Nexus is the core reasoning engine.

Responsibilities​

  • Interpret problem frames from Bethy
  • Plan and generate solutions using:
    • LLMs
    • Tools (data access, integrations)
    • Knowledge Graph queries
  • Produce structured outputs:
    • Plans
    • Checklists
    • Refactored workflows
    • Recommended interventions

It is designed to model neurodivergent workflows, not just generic “tasks.”

Neunuc: Adaptive Roadmap Review (ARR) Loop​

The Neunuc ARR Loop implements an ongoing planning cycle.

Responsibilities​

  • Continuously assess:
    • Current tasks and incidents
    • Calendar load
    • User-supplied energy and cognitive state
  • Reconcile short-term and long-term goals
  • Propose updates to:
    • Priorities
    • Scopes
    • Timelines
  • Feed back into the Knowledge Graph as “learning” from each cycle

Neunuc effectively provides executive function as a service.

Neuro-Navigator​

The Neuro-Navigator is focused on neurodivergent & trauma-aware support.

Note: This system is not a replacement for clinical care. It is designed for support and structure, not diagnosis or therapy.

Responsibilities​

  • Interpret the user’s neuro_profile and context
  • Adapt all outputs to:
    • Break down tasks into manageable steps
    • Minimize decision fatigue
    • Reduce context-switching
  • Offer supportive structures such as:
    • Co-working style flows
    • Scripts for difficult conversations
    • Recovery-aware pacing suggestions

Ethos Navigator​

The Ethos Navigator ensures verifiable trust and adaptive culture.

Responsibilities​

  • Enforce rules of:
    • What the system is allowed to access
    • What it’s allowed to automate
    • When explicit approval is required
  • Maintain:
    • Audit logs
    • Action rationales
    • Policy versions
  • Apply “culture configs” such as:
    • Preferred tone ranges
    • Neurodivergent-friendly norms
    • Communication boundaries

Ethos Navigator is the ethical spine of Godmode.

Humor SOP: Stereotype & Identity Operational Stack​

The Humor SOP encodes how humor and identity are handled.

Responsibilities​

  • Respect user’s preferences for:
    • Tone & language
    • Use and limits of humor
  • Avoid:
    • Harmful stereotypes
    • Tokenization or fetishization of neurodivergence
  • Use humor as a functional tool:
    • Reduce shame
    • Interrupt negative spirals
    • Normalize cognitive variance without trivializing it

Humor SOP works closely with Bethy, Neuro-Navigator, and Ethos Navigator to ensure that communication is both effective and safe.
